Enterprise Analytics Applications
Informed Visibility (IV)
Provides near real-time information, insights and expanded visibility as mail moves through the mailstream.
Enterprise Transportation Analytics (ETA)
Provides location analytics and visualization of USPS trucks, trailers, and private/commercial airline assets moving the mail.
The Delivery Management System (DMS)
Assists delivery managers in making near real time decisions that positively affect workhours and customer delivery windows.
Product Tracking & Reporting (PTR)
Provides tracking data for package visibility and service performance reporting.
SVWeb
Provides real time transportation updates and reporting on the movement of trailers in the surface network.
Volume Arrival Profile (VAP)
Provides a specific set of tools and utilities which support USPS daily operational needs.
USPS National Map
Provides visualizations, analysis and data modeling to aid in strategic decision making for postal operations.
Address Management System (AMS)
Enables mail processing automation, by maintaining the addressing data used by all automated equipment.
Service and Field Operations Performance Measurement
Provides service analysis reporting and assessments.

Enterprise Analytics Team

Our mission is to provide innovative data-driven solutions that focus on achieving end-to-end visibility to support the USPS and our customers. By utilizing advanced modeling and geospatial tools we are moving from descriptive and diagnostic problem-solving to predictive and prescriptive analytics.

Informed Visibility (IV) provides near real-time information, insights and expanded visibility as mail moves through the mailstream.

The Product Tracking & Reporting (PTR) system provides tracking data for package visibility and service performance reporting.

USPS National Map provides visualizations, analysis and data modeling to aid in strategic decision making for postal operations.

Enterprise Transportation Analytics (ETA) provides location analytics and visualization of USPS trucks, trailers, and private/commercial airline assets moving the mail.

SVWeb provides real time transportation updates and reporting on the movement of trailers in the surface network.

The Address Management System (AMS) enables mail processing automation, by maintaining the addressing data used by all automated equipment.

The Delivery Management System (DMS) assists delivery managers in making near real time decisions that positively affect workhours and customer delivery windows.

The Volume Arrival Profile (VAP) provides a specific set of tools and utilities which support USPS daily operational needs.

The Service and Field Operations Performance Measurement system provides service analysis reporting and assessments.
